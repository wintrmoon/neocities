a long yap about paper and pens because why not

read more

I'm kinda a snob when it comes to anything stationary related, like it has to be A5 dotted cream paper from leucthrum, sorry I'm not writing anything down on your cheap ass post-it notes

my love for writing and documenting things is only partially the writing part, what I love the most about it is just the experience of sitting down with my pen or keyboard and experimenting,,, trying to draw weird shapes with a brush pen or trying out a new program to write with

I have experimented with a lot of different pens okay that's a lie, I used to write a lot with bic pens but a couple of years ago I tried zebra sarasa clip pens and fell in love, writing with the right pen and paper is a night and day difference, writing is just so much more fun when it's smooth

I bought a rotring 600 pen to try something different,,, and I hate to say that I don't like it as much, I freaking love the design and it's the best feeling pen I have ever written with but my writing just looks smoother when writing with a sarasa pen,,, I'm gonna try a different refill and see how that goes, it's kinda funny that a pen 10 times the price doesn't write as good ,,, to be honest I only bought that pen because I like the design of it

just as I'm obsessed with pens, I am also obsessed with digital writing tools,,, I recently discovered writerdecks (also check out cyberdecks), just imagine a very bad laptop that you can only use to write with, it's amazing,,, most people make their own diy decks, it kinda reminds me of the terminal in the fallout games,,, I currently don't really want to spend the money on something like that but the next best thing is just using WordGrinder with a clicky mechanical keyboard

anyway so my point is uhh that uh hmm,,, I just wanted to yap about my stationary obsession, I didn't even get the chance to talk about notebooks,,, so my point is; make writing a fun hobby by making the process fun, the end result doesn't really matter
