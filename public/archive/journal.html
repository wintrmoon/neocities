29/06/2025 - The least known artist I listen to

Youniss is one of the most underrated artists I listen to. If I have to recommend one song it’s simply Escaper. the sounds and his general style as an artist blends so well together. the arabic influences combined with the noisy production are something I never heard before.

I was very lucky to see him live (technically I was working, I just heard him from afar) but you can imagine that his music hits differently live, I won’t forget that day. I’m quite amazed seeing he doesn’t have more followers, even locally I haven’t heard his name much.

my personal favourite is Walad, it’s a noisy adventure that somehow makes my head very happy! he’s seriously slept on, go listen!
